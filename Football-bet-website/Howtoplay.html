How To Play

Game Description

Paris Publics is a platform where football enthusiasts can test their knowledge and have fun by challenging each other on match outcomes. You can challenge an opponent on either the overall result (winner/draw) or the exact score of a match.

How to Play

  • You must pay a stake of 1 Bronze Star to create a challenge for the overall result. For an exact score challenge, both players pay 2Bronze Stars.
  • If you correctly predict the overall result, you win your opponent's stake.
  • If you correctly predict the exact score, you win twice your opponent's stake (your opponent's stake + an equal amount from us).
  • If you create a challenge and no one accepts it, you will get your stake back, minus a small fee of 1 or 2 Bronze Stars, depending on the bet type.
  • If both you and your opponent predict the wrong overall result, you both lose your stakes.
  • If both you and your opponent predict the wrong exact score, you both get your stakes back.
  • Bets must be placed at least 3 hours before the start of the match; after that, they will be closed.

How to Get Bronze Stars

  • Betting against friends: Wagers can range from 1 to 120 Bronze Stars, depending on your available balance.
  • Football quizzes: Earn O to 10 Bronze Stars per quiz, available once every 6 hours. Each quiz consists of 10 multiple-choice questions. You win one Bronze Star for each correct answer, allowing you to earn up to 10 Bronze Stars per quiz.
  • Daily spin: Spin the wheel once every 24 hours to win 0 to 10 Bronze Stars.
  • Watching ads: Earn 20 Bronze Stars per ad, watchable once every 4 hours.
  • 4 Goals Game Earn 0 to 10 Bronze Stars per game, playable once every 2
  • "Goals scored" indicates the number of digits you've guessed correctly and placed in the correct position.
  • "Goals missed" indicates the number of digits you've guessed correctly but placed in the wrong position.
  • You start with 10 Bronze Stars.
  • For each incorrect attempt (i.e., not guessing all four digits correctly), you lose one Bronze Star.
  • The game ends when you guess the correct combination or exhaust your Bronze Stars.

Bet Types:

    You can set public or private bets:

  • Public bets: Anyone can bet against you.
  • Private bets: You can share it and play against only a friend that you shared the link of your bet with.

Gifts and Rewards:

We offer various gift cards, T-shirts, and other types of gifts, subject to availability in your country. You can still collect Bronze Stars; once gifts are available in your country, you can redeem them.

Gifts are earned with Gold Stars, which can only be obtained by converting Bronze Stars. The conversion rate is 1000 Bronze Stars = 1 1 Gold Star.

Important Note: This site is not a gambling site. It is a platform for football enthusiasts to test their knowledge and have fun. The site will never ask for or provide real money; gifts are symbolic rewards for loyal and passionate users.

We invite you to enjoy the experience without cheating or attempting to gain stars through illegal methods. The site reserves the right to block your account if any illegal actions are detected.
